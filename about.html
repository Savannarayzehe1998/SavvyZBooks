About Me

Hello all,

I am Savanna Ray Zehe. I’m an avid reader, originally from the evergreen, ever-cold state of Washington, now residing in sunny, sandy-beached Florida. My one constant in life has always been my love of reading, so here I am, creating an online website to curate all my favorite fantasy books that will help you escape this world. Please enjoy this website, and better yet, enjoy my recommendations and links to reading that’s out of this world.
