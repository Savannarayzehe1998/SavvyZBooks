My Interests

My interests extend far beyond my passion for reading. I love volunteering at the local animal shelter and fostering puppies until they find their perfect homes. I'm also a musician, playing both the bass and ukulele. I have a great love for comedy and enjoy writing comedic music. When I'm not working, reading, or at the animal shelter, you can find me and my beloved dog on nature walks.
